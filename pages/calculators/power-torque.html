Practical guidance

Engineering Notes

Design tips

Size drives for peak duty, starting torque, service factor, and thermal limits—not nominal power alone.

Safety notes

Guard rotating shafts and couplings. Verify overspeed, shaft stress, and emergency-stop requirements.

Common mistakes

Do not mix kW with W, rpm with rad/s, or input power with useful shaft output.

Best efficiency ranges

Well-sized industrial motors commonly operate most efficiently around 75–100% of rated load.
