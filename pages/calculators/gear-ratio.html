Worked Example

Given

Driver teeth = 20, driven teeth = 60, driver speed = 1750 rpm, driver torque = 120 Nm, efficiency = 95%.

Formula

i = Ndriven / Ndriver   Tout = Tin × i × η

Substitution

i = 60 / 20 = 3   Tout = 120 × 3 × 0.95 = 342 Nm.

Answer

The gear train reduces speed by a factor of 3 and increases torque to approximately 342 Nm at 95% efficiency.
